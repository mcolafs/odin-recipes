Tasty breakfast pancakes

Mouth watering pancakes. Fast and simple.

Breakfast pancakes

    Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Wheat flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs and milk
  2. Add flour, sugar and baking powder to the mix
  3. Add melted and tibid butter
  4. Let it rest 20 minutes
  5. Heat frying pan with butter or oil
  6. Fry until brown on both sides
  7. Serve with fruits, marmelade, nutella or yogurt
