Classic and delicious lasagna

This is a simple and classic Lasagna. Easy to make and a real crowd pleaser.

  • Canned tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Pasta plates
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Minced meat
  • Tomato paste
  • Milk
  • Wheat flour
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  • Add chopped onionas and garlic to a lightly heated pan. Fry it until it goes blank and soft.
  • In another pan, fry the minced meat until i gets brown and caramelized
  • Add garlic and onion to the meat. Add canned tomatoes and tomato paste and let it simmer.
  • Melt butter and add flour. Stir while heating.
  • Add milk and let it simmer until it thickens.
  • Add tomato sauce, bechamel sauce and pasta plates in layers.
  • Add grated cheese on top and let it cook for 40 minutes
  • Serve with salad and garlic bread
